# Oatz — Overnight Oats Brand

## Concept Overview
**Oatz** is a branded overnight oats product and CPG food company. The idea is to build a recognizable, consumer-facing brand around ready-to-make or ready-to-eat overnight oats — positioned as a healthy, convenient, and craveable breakfast option.

Collab with **Kane**. Separate from solo [[rare-force-one|RARE FORCE ONE]] projects. See also: [[ventures-with-kane]]

---

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** Oatz
- **Tone:** Clean, modern, energetic — health-forward but not pretentious
- **Target Market:** Health-conscious millennials/Gen Z, busy people who want a quick nutritious breakfast
- **Positioning:** Simple ingredients, real flavors, no fuss

---

## Product Ideas
- **Classic Oatz** — vanilla, honey, or plain base with mix-in options
- **Chocolate Oatz** — cocoa + banana or peanut butter variant
- **Tropical Oatz** — mango, coconut, pineapple
- **Protein Oatz** — higher-protein version for fitness crowd
- **Seasonal drops** — pumpkin spice, peppermint, etc.

*Format options: dry mix kit, pre-portioned jar, single-serve cup*
