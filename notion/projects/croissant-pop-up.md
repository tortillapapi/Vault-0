# Croissant Pop-Up

## Concept Overview
A pop-up food business centered on creative, fusion-style croissants. The concept blends elevated pastry culture with bold flavor combinations — starting with Mexican-inspired and street food crossovers. Think approachable, Instagrammable, and delicious.

**Core idea:** Take the croissant format and push it somewhere unexpected — culturally familiar but visually exciting.

Collab with **Kane**. Separate from solo [[rare-force-one|RARE FORCE ONE]] projects. See also: [[ventures-with-kane]]

---

## Menu Ideas

### Concha Croissant
Mexican concha bread × croissant fusion. Flaky croissant layers with a sweet, colored concha sugar crust on top. Multiple concha shell flavors: vanilla, chocolate, strawberry.

### Stuffed Meat Croissant Pockets
Deep-fried croissant dough pockets stuffed with seasoned meat. Think birria, al pastor, or picadillo fillings. Served with dipping sauce / consomme.
