# Pokemon Market Sentiment Scraper

## Overview
Use NotebookLM or OpenClaw to aggregate and analyze Pokemon content across key platforms — Reddit, Twitter/X, YouTube, news sites — and surface market sentiment signals for Pokemon card products and sets. The goal is to identify hype cycles, anticipated releases, and reprint rumors *before* the market moves, giving [[rare-force-one|RARE FORCE ONE]] an informational edge.

**Stage:** Idea / Early Planning

---

## Goals
- Aggregate Pokemon content from key sources (r/PokemonTCG, Twitter/X, PokeBeach, TCGPlayer, YouTube)
- Generate sentiment summaries to flag trending cards, sets, and products
- Produce actionable buy/sell/hold signals for RARE FORCE ONE Pokemon inventory
- Eventually automate daily/weekly sentiment reports

---

## Current Status
**Idea / Early Planning** — Concept stage only. No active development yet. Needs feasibility scoping.

Key questions to answer before proceeding:
- Can OpenClaw natively scrape web sources, or does it need an API?

- What's the MVP — manual pull vs. automated feed?
