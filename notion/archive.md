# Archive

Cold storage. Completed projects, finished tasks, reviewed notes. Nothing here demands your attention.

## Archived Projects
Open the [[projects-database|Projects Database]] → switch to the "Archived Projects" view to see all completed and archived projects.

## Completed Tasks
Open the [[task-database|Task Database]] → switch to the "Completed Tasks" view for done tasks.

## Reviewed Notes
Open the [[notes-database|Notes Database]] → switch to the "Reviewed Notes" view for processed notes.

> *Archive liberally. A full Archive is a sign of a productive system, not a cluttered one.*
