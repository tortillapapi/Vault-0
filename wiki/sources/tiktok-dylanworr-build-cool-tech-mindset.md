## Summary

A motivational/mindset clip: stop taking advice from people who flex cool-looking
tech but never reveal "the sauce," and instead follow people who actually teach
you *how* to build. No tools or steps — it's a philosophy/curation reminder.

## Key Points

1. Distrust pure-demo flexing without method.
2. Curate your inputs toward creators who explain the how.
